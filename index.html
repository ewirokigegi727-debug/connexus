Why members choose Connexus

A complete financial relationship in one place

Connexus highlights the combination members usually have to piece together from several institutions: nationally available membership, digital access, competitive rates, and a growing network of branch and ATM access. The credit union emphasizes higher yields on select checking, savings, certificates, and retirement products while also offering mortgages, vehicle loans, personal lending, and card solutions that support day-to-day life. The result is a straightforward experience where earning, borrowing, and managing money stay connected.
