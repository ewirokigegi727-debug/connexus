Deposit lineup

Everyday access plus savings that can grow

Connexus positions its deposit products to cover routine spending, short-term reserves, and longer savings horizons. Public information highlights free checking options, higher-yield checking tiers, money market flexibility, health savings accounts for qualified medical expenses, retirement products for future planning, and certificate choices for members who want predictable growth over a set term. Instead of separating convenience from yield, the product strategy is designed to let members manage transactions, save intentionally, and keep a clear view of progress in digital banking.

Borrowing options

Loan solutions for vehicles, homes, education, and personal goals

Lending at Connexus spans vehicles, mortgages, refinance options, home equity access, unsecured borrowing, and student financing. That breadth matters because members rarely need just one product over time. A family might start with auto financing, refinance later, tap home equity for renovations, and eventually compare retirement cash-flow options. Connexus organizes these solutions so members can identify the right structure for each milestone while staying within the same cooperative ecosystem for service and support.
