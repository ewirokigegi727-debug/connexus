Official contact methods

Phone, mailing, and secure support channels

Connexus emphasizes secure, authenticated contact methods for account-specific help. The member contact center phone line remains the primary direct option for most questions, while secure messaging inside Digital Banking is the recommended path for private account discussions. Public website content also points members toward live chat, scheduled calls, and branch visits depending on the issue. Notably, Connexus does not prominently publish a general support email for sensitive inquiries, which aligns with a security-first approach that keeps personal account data out of less protected channels.

Business hours and support flow

Choose the right channel for the right request

Public contact guidance indicates that members should call or use secure digital channels for urgent or sensitive questions, especially around cards, account access, and transfers. Branch visits work well for location-based service needs, while chat and call scheduling help when members want guided conversations without waiting in line. Reported member contact center hours are Monday through Wednesday and Friday from 7 AM to 7 PM CT, Thursday from 9 AM to 7 PM CT, and Saturday from 8 AM to 1 PM CT, with Sunday closed. Branch-specific hours should always be confirmed on the official location pages before visiting.

Connexus does not prominently publish a general support email on its official site. For security, use secure messaging in Digital Banking or call the member center.
